Blessed Arms Charity Home

A Safe Haven of Hope for Vulnerable Children

INTRODUCTION

At Blessed Arms Charity Home, every child matters. Located in the heart of Gayaza Subcounty, Kiwenda, our home is a safe and loving haven for vulnerable children who need care, support, and a second chance at life

We currently provide shelter, education, and emaotional support to 23 amazing children including 5 from disadvantaged families. This helps them to grow in a nurturing environment filled with hope and compassion.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to give every child the love and opportunities they deserve. At Blessed Arms, we believe that kindness can change lives and together, we are building brighter futures, one child at a time

ELDERLY CARE

Here at Blessed Arms Charity, we are also dedicated to providing essential assistance to elderly people in need, focusing on their most basic yet vital requirements. Our primary goal is to ensure that our elderly friends receive the care and support they truly deserve, particularly in times of hardships. We provide food, as many struggle to access it.

These small acts of kindness can have great impact on the lives of the elderly. Your contribution will help us to provide more food and support to individuals and expand our services to reach even more people who require assistance. Together, we can bring joy, comfort and hope to people need it the most

FEED A CHILD, CHANGE A FUTURE

Every day, countless children go to bed hungry, uncertain of where the next meal will come from. Hunger robs them of good, health, interrupts their education, and reduces their chance to a bright future they deserve. We cn change that together.

In Matthew 25:40, Jesus says "Truly I tell you, whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me." Feeding children in need is more than a charity, it is a powerful act of love and kindness towards the most vulnerable among us

Your support can provide food, hope, and a chance at a better tomorrow for these precious children. Even a smallest group can make a lasting relationship in a children's life.
