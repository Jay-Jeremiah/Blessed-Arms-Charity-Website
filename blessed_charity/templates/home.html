Blessed Arms Charity Home

A Safe Haven of Hope for Vulnerable Children

INTRODUCTION

At Blessed Arms Charity Home, every child matters. Located in the heart of Gayaza Subcounty, Kiwenda, our home is a safe and loving haven for vulnerable children who need care, support, and a second chance at life.

We currently provide shelter, education, and emotional support to 23 amazing children including 5 from disadvantaged families. This helps them grow in a nurturing environment filled with hope and compassion.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to give every child the love and opportunities they deserve. At Blessed Arms, we believe that kindness can change lives and together, we are building brighter futures, one child at a time.

Here at Blessed Arms Charity, we are also dedicated to providing essential assistance to elderly people in need, focusing on their most basic yet vital requirements. Our primary goal is to ensure that our elderly friends receive the care and support they deserve, particularly in times of hardships. We provide food, as many struggle to access it.

These small acts of kindness can have impact on the lives of the elderly. Your contribution will help us to provide more food support individuals and expand our services to reach even more people who require assistance. Together, we can bring joy, comfort and hope to people who need it the most.
