Our Vision

To establish a society where underpriviledged children have access to safe, affordable housing and can reach their full potenetial, ultimately creating a world free from poverty, homelessness, and inequality, where the children can live with dignity and purpose.

Our Mission

To offer a safe, nurturing environment where these children can grow, learn and thrive. We believe every child deserves acces to basic necessities, education, and love. At Blessed Arms Charity Home, we're commited to making a positive impact in the lives of these young ones.

Meet Our Dedicated Team

At Blessed Arms Charity Home, we are deeply grateful for the unwavering dedication and compassion of our incredible team. Day in and day out, these selfless individuals go above and beyond to provide love, care, and support to the children who call our home their own.

Our team is the heartbeat of our mission who work tirelessly to ensure that every child feels safe, nurtured, and valued. Whether it is through offering emotional support, guiding educational journeys, or simply sharing a warm smile, each team member plays a vital role in shaping brighter futures for the children in our care.

We celebrate and honor their commitment, their patience, and the countless ways they pour their hearts into creating a home full of hope, healing, and happiness.

Our Team includes:

  • Mrs. Katende: Caretaker
  • Mrs. Nakawuma: Caretaker

These two amazing women are more than just caretakers, they are like mothers to our children. With love, patience, and kindness, they care for each child as their own. Every day, they make sure the children are safe, happy, and growing up in a warm, loving home. We are so grateful for their big hearts and the joy they bring to Blessed Arms Charity Home.
